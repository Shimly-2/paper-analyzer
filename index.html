Envisioning Beyond the Pixels: Benchmarking Reasoning-Informed Visual Editing

Abstract

We present RISEBench, a comprehensive benchmark for evaluating reasoning-informed visual editing capabilities in multimodal models. Our benchmark introduces three key evaluation dimensions: Instruction Reasoning, Appearance Consistency, and Visual Plausibility.

1. Introduction

Visual editing tasks require models to understand both natural language instructions and visual content. However, existing benchmarks focus primarily on output quality without evaluating the reasoning process.

We propose a multi-dimensional scoring system that provides fine-grained analysis of model capabilities across different aspects of visual reasoning.
